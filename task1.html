About bike

Bikes are used for a wide range of activities, including commuting, recreation, exercise, and competitive sports. They are an eco-friendly and efficient mode of transportation.

About car

A car (also known as an automobile) is a motorized vehicle designed for the transportation of people and goods. Typically, cars have four wheels and are powered by an internal combustion engine (though electric cars use electric motors). Cars are commonly used for personal travel, commuting, and commercial purposes.

About train

A train is a series of connected vehicles that run along a railway track and are used for the transportation of passengers or freight.Trains are typically powered by locomotives (either diesel or electric), and they can carry large numbers of people or goods over long distances. They are a vital mode of transport in many countries, particularly for mass transit and heavy freight.

About aeroplane

An airplane (or aeroplane in British English) is a powered flying vehicle with fixed wings, designed to transport passengers, cargo, or both through the air.It is one of the most common forms of modern transportation for long-distance travel and is crucial for global travel, trade, and communication.
